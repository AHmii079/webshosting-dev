Welcome to HostingWebservers.com

We’re not just a hosting company. We’re hosting.com. HostingWebservers.com is the new home of A2 Hosting and a global platform built on one thing: trust.

Our ethos

Whether you're building your first site or managing hundreds, we're here to help you grow with transparency, speed, and support that shows up before you need it.

  • Our vision

    To be the most trusted hosting company in the world. That’s it. That’s our North Star. At HostingWebservers.com, we’re building something better: reliable, sustainable, and unapologetically human. Trust is earned daily. Not just through uptime, but by doing what we say, fixing what breaks, and showing up when it matters.

    Our leadership doesn’t sit on the sidelines. We build fast, lead by example, and go the extra mile. We believe in quiet confidence, taking action, and helping entrepreneurs win worldwide.
